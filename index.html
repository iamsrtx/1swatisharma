The Secret Sauce is You

Being Your 'Ownmate'

The Secret Sauce is You (TSSIY) is my debut book.

For all this while, I thought heaven and hell are places.

Until one day, I unexpectedly and abruptly hit rock bottom. In a situation like this, we usually play this funny blame game. So, bruised and bleeding, I whined and blamed that heaven and hell are not places but people.

Slowly I learned that my demarcation of heaven and hell was around the people I enveloped my life with. By this logic, I was placing the very people in my life at the zenith level since I was attaching my happiness or misery to them.

Darkness, silence and solitude at rock bottom made me question this blame game. I was seeking some solace from and a lot more! Questions are indeed the answers! And it dawned upon me that putting other on the pedestal was a mistake. Gradually, I learned that I had to unlearn (a lot) to learn the lessions in the finest Gurukul of life called rock-bottom.

From hereon began my quest for someone who isn't outside of me to curate my definition of heaven and hell. I began to look for someone who isn't detached from me- literally and metamorphically.

Standing at this crossroad of my life, I had umpteen choices of people to choose from- Yes, people again! While some may call it luck, I call it dark humour! Good sense prevailed, and I chose to choose none of them as they were all outside of me.

But I had to make a choice. I wasm after all, standing at the crossroad of my life. Finally, I chose......
